01

Overview

Project Gunning Wild (working title) is a 2D Shooting-Gallery game with Arcade mechanics.
You play as a cowboy and you shoot down enemies/bosses in the town just to see if you are stronger than them.

02

Story

Bored by the monotony of exploring the desert, you wander through different towns in search of improving your shooting skills, whilst running out bandits from local areas. This story takes place in the western lands of the USA, where your name will bring fear to the enemies.

03

Concept

Eliminate local town bandits and their boss using your revolver. Rack up a combo by chaining your shots, from there you will gather more coins to spend in the item shop. The item shop is a great way to upgrade gear.
