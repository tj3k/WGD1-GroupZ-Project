03

Concept

To start the game, you enter a town and then take over it by shooting at the residents building after building.
Once you're done with the small-fry, you have to face the town "boss".
You aim with your mouse and you shoot with the mouse click as you would on a first person game.
You'll be able to rack up a combo if you manage to chain a string of shots on an enemy or between others.
This will increase your total score for the level.
After a while, you will encounter a shop where you can freshen up/upgrade your gear for later in the game.
